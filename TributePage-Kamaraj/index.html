The Timeline of Kamarajar

  • 1903 : Born Kumaraswami Kamaraj on july 15, in Virudhunagar, Tamil Nadu.

  • 1914 : Dropped out of school to support his mother

  • 1921 : Joined Congress as a full-ltime political worker,attended the public meeting and met Gandhi for the first time at madhurai.

  • 1930 : Kamaraj went to jail for two years for participating in the "Salt Satyagraha".

  • 1937 : At the age of 34, Kamaraj entered the Assembly winning the Sattur seat in the 1937 election.

  • 1954 : Kamaraj became the Chief Minister of Madras Province.

  • 1959 : Kamaraj and Bishnuram Medhi (Governor) took efforts to establish IIT Madras.

  • 1963 : He resigned from the post of the Chief Minister. He proposed that all senior Congress leaders should resign from their posts and devote all their energy to the re-vitalization of the Congress

  • 1969 : Kamaraj became the leader of the Indian National Congress.

  • 13 April 1954 to 2 October 1963 : Golden Era of Kamarajar Period
